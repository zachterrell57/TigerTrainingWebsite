What do I need to bring?

  • Cool, athletic clothing
  • Tennis shoes

  • Introductory Strength Progressions

  • Multi-Directional Agilities
  • Force Production
  • Force Absorption
  • Skill-developing games and drills

Future Tigers entering grades 5-8 in Fall 2020 (both boys and girls are welcome)
