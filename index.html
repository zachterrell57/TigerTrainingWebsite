The coaching staff at Blue Valley High School is very proud and excited to offer this opportunity for 5th-8th grade male and female student athletes who will one day wear the Black and Gold of the Tigers!

July 7 - August 6

Football Practice Field – South of the Tennis Courts
Blue Valley High School
6001 W. 159th Street

Tuesdays and Thursdays
10:00 - 10:45 AM

(In the case of large enrollment, we will add a session at 10:45 and
separate by grade level)

Cost

  • $160 Total
  • 5 Weeks (10 Training Sessions)
  • Tiger Training T-Shirt
  • Make checks payable to "Tiger Training"

What do I need to bring?

  • Cool, athletic clothing
  • Tennis shoes or cleats
  • Water Bottle (athletes must have their own)

What will the training consist of?

  • Introductory Strength Progressions
  • Speed Development - Form/Technique
  • Multi-Directional Agilities
  • Force Production
  • Force Absorption
  • Skill-developing games and drills

Who can register?

Future Tigers entering grades 5-8 in Fall 2020 (both boys and girls are welcome)

Register and pay by cash or check July 7 (1st Training Session)

Two makeup days - 8/3 and 8/5 to make up an absence for any reason (other camps, vacation, etc.)
